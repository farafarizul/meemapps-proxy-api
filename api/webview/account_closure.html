Important Information for Account Closure

Before proceeding, users should review the account closure requirements carefully. Closing the account is final and will permanently end related payout incentives, bonuses, and inherited benefits.

Before you proceed
  • Please ensure you have read and understood the terms and conditions for account closure.
  • Closing your account will permanently remove and terminate any payout incentives, bonuses, and other benefits you or your nominator should receive and inherit.
  • KBSE Bullion Sdn. Bhd. will not be responsible for any future claims regarding this account by you or any other parties.
